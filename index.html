Explore UC Santa Cruz

How to:

Use your arrow keys or WASD to move around the map. When you walk near a landmark, scroll down to see it's name, photo, and description. If you would like to contribute and add your own landmark, click on the link at the bottom that says "Submit your own photo for this location." It will take you to a Google Form that you can fill out and submit. When a team member has reviewed your entry and uploaded the data into our database, your entry will show up! It may take a while since our team members are busy, so please be patient if you do not see your entry. üôè Please note that any incomplete or inappropriate submissions will not pass the review check and thus will not be allowed on the map.
